TIMELINE

ROLE

Design, Development

SKILLS

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The team’s objective was to replicate a pragmatic environment in which BMC residents can practice robotic surgical procedures, including successful modeling of anatomically precise organs.

The motivation for successful model completion is to establish a cost effective manufacturing method to current models that are expensive and show rapid deterioration with use.

To date, the team has successfully built a full working prototype for the Nissen Fundoplication procedure. The BMC client has demonstrated working PoC on the final prototype. The physical prototype is coupled with detailed manufacturing procedures for each individual unit.

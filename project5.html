EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The objective of this project was to design and produce a simple, effective solution for leveling tables in restaurants with uneven floors. The tables are assumed to be square with the ground, and with firmly attached, circular legs. The wobble is assumed to be caused by height differences in the points of the floor that the legs sit on, not the construction of the table itself. For the purpose of this project, the maximum height difference is determined to be 0.5 inches and the application is limited to indoor use. It must be easily adjustable by restaurant employees, and must allow the area under the table to be cleaned easily. Lastly, the product must cost less than $50 per table and we have 2 week to complete a prototype a present a demo.

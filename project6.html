EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The proliferation of 3D printed products in recent years, particularly in lighting design, has opened new possibilities for creative manufacturing. I'm particularly drawn to how designers use 3D printing to create textured lampshades that diffuse light in compelling ways. This project aimed to develop a textured 3D printed lampshade that both celebrates the manufacturing process and creates beautiful, soft light diffusion.

During my research into existing 3D printed lighting solutions, I discovered FullControl—a Python-based software that generates custom G-code for additive manufacturing. A secondary objective became learning to leverage the FullControl GitHub repository to create custom G-code, enabling shapes and textures that would be unfeasible through traditional CAD modeling and slicing workflows.

The design inspiration for the Hogyo Lamp draws from traditional Japanese architecture and the aesthetic principles of the brand Wooj.

The resulting lamp successfully demonstrates both the aesthetic potential and technical capabilities of custom G-code 3D printing.

ABOUT ME

Mechanical design engineer specializing in product development, CAD modeling, and prototyping. Experienced in designing mechanical solutions that integrate seamlessly with industrial design while ensuring manufacturability and cost efficiency. Skilled in developing 3D CAD models, creating proof-of-concept prototypes, and refining designs for production. Knowledgeable in mass production processes (injection molding, CNC machining, metal casting, etc.) and supplier collaboration to bring products from concept to reality.

Known for hands-on problem-solving, iterative design refinement, and effectively managing concurrent projects. Excels in cross-functional collaboration with designers, engineers, clients, and manufacturers. Passionate about creating innovative, user-centered products while fostering a detail-oriented and team-driven design process.
